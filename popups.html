WEEKEND POP-UPS

Jesse’s BBQ offers rotating weekend pop-up menus with smoked favorites, seasonal specials, and limited preorder pickup.

HOW POP-UPS WORK

Each weekend, Jesse’s BBQ posts a changing menu for Saturday and Sunday pickup. Customers preorder in advance, and menus may vary depending on what is being smoked that week.

  • Preorders are required for weekend pop-ups
  • Orders must be placed by Thursday at 6:00 PM
  • Pickup is available Saturday and Sunday
  • Pickup details are shared with each weekly menu
  • Payment is completed through SumUp

WHAT TO EXPECT

Weekend menus may include smoked brisket, ribs, chicken, sandwiches, sides, jalapeño poppers, and other rotating specials. Seasonal items like crawfish may also be available at certain times of the year.
